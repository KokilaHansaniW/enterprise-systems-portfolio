# Data Protection Impact Assessment (Lite)

## Data Processed
- Patient internal ID
- Clinician ID
- Appointment date and time
- Status and notes

No NHS numbers or national identifiers are stored.

## Risks
- Unauthorised access
- Data leakage
- Excessive retention

## Mitigations
- Role-based access control
- Audit logging
- Encryption at rest (SQL Server)
- Defined retention policy
